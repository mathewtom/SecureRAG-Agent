# Approval Matrix — Effective 2025-01-01 (SUPERSEDED 2026-01-01)

Historical reference. The current matrix is `approval_matrix_2026.md`.
The most material change for 2026 was the introduction of CEO countersign
on contracts above $100k; under the 2025 matrix the CFO could sign
unilaterally up to $250k.

## Expense reports

| Amount | Approver |
|---|---|
| Up to $500 | Manager |
| $501 – $5,000 | Director |
| $5,001 – $25,000 | VP / function head |
| $25,001 – $250,000 | CFO (sole signature) |
| Above $250,000 | CFO + CEO |

## Vendor and SaaS contracts

| Annual contract value | Approver |
|---|---|
| Up to $25,000 | Director (no security review required if vendor was on the pre-approved list) |
| $25,001 – $250,000 | CFO (sole signature) + Security review |
| $250,001 – $1,000,000 | CFO + CEO + Security review |
| Above $1,000,000 | CFO + CEO + GC review + Board notification |

## Headcount

| Action | Approver |
|---|---|
| Backfill within approved plan | Manager + HR |
| New role within approved plan | Director + HR |
| New role outside approved plan | VP + CFO |
| Director or above hire | CEO + COO |

## Settlements and legal

| Action | Approver |
|---|---|
| Routine vendor dispute under $50k | GC |
| Customer settlement under $500k | GC + CFO |
| Customer settlement above $500k | GC + CFO + CEO |
| Any government investigation response | GC + CEO |

## Why the 2026 update

A Q3 2025 Audit Committee finding noted that single-signature authority
above $100k concentrated risk and was inconsistent with peer-company
practice. The Board recommended the change; it took effect with the
2026 fiscal year.

# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

Owned by People Operations (E044). This is the operational checklist;
the policy context is in `hr_handbook.md`.

## Day 0 (one week before start)

- [ ] Hardware ordered (HR ticket type `it`, default to standard MacBook
      Pro unless role-specific exception)
- [ ] Email and SSO accounts provisioned
- [ ] Manager assigned an onboarding buddy from the team
- [ ] Calendar invites sent for first-week onboarding sessions

## Day 1

- [ ] Welcome session with People Operations
- [ ] Code of Conduct and Acceptable Use Policy acknowledgement
- [ ] Hardware setup, MDM enrollment, security key issuance (if
      `clearance_level >= 3`)
- [ ] Manager 1:1 — role expectations and 30/60/90 plan
- [ ] Slack profile and team channels

## Week 1

- [ ] Annual security training (`security_training_2026.md`)
- [ ] Department-specific tooling walkthrough
- [ ] Shadow at least one cross-functional meeting

## Week 2–4

- [ ] First measurable deliverable identified
- [ ] Org-chart review with manager (`org_chart.md`) — who to know,
      who to ask what
- [ ] Probationary check-in with HR (E026)

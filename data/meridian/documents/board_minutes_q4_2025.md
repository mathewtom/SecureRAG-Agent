# Board Minutes — Q4 2025 Meeting

**Date:** 2025-11-13
**Format:** Hybrid, San Francisco office
**Attendees:** Board (3 independent directors), CEO Derek Washington, CFO
Rachel Goldstein, GC James Okafor. Other executives attended for
relevant items.

## Agenda

1. Q3 2025 financial results
2. Audit Committee report — including the finding that drove the
   2026 approval matrix change (single-signature authority above $100k
   was identified as concentrating risk)
3. 2026 budget direction
4. Phoenix migration status (cutover completed 2025-11-15; postmortem
   pending — see `project_phoenix_postmortem.md`)
5. Executive session

## Key resolutions

- Endorsed the Audit Committee recommendation to require CEO countersign
  on contracts above $100k starting 2026 fiscal year. Counsel and
  Finance directed to update the approval matrix accordingly.
- Approved 2026 headcount targets in principle, pending final budget
  ratification at Q1 2026 meeting.
- Acknowledged Phoenix migration as a successful execution despite
  late-stage incidents; directed Engineering to complete the postmortem
  before declaring the project closed.

## Executive session

Closed.

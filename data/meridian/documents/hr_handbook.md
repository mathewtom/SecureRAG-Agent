# Employee Handbook

This handbook codifies how things actually run at Meridian. It is a living
document; check the `last_updated` field above for currency.

## Working norms

- **Default working hours.** 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. local time, with a one-hour
  unpaid lunch. Remote employees follow the same expectation in their
  declared time zone.
- **Time zones of record.** Each employee has a `location` in the directory
  used to determine standing meeting times. Cross-time-zone meetings should
  rotate to share the burden.
- **Communication.** Slack for synchronous, email for cross-company,
  documents for anything that needs a paper trail. Decisions made in
  Slack must be summarized in a document or ticket.

## Time off

- Twenty paid vacation days per calendar year, accrued monthly.
- Twelve sick days per year, no accrual cap.
- Twelve weeks of paid parental leave for primary caregiver, six weeks for
  secondary caregiver.
- Bereavement, jury duty, and military service follow statute.

Vacation requests of one week or more require manager pre-approval at
least two weeks in advance. Shorter requests follow team norms.

## Performance

Two formal performance reviews per year (May and November) plus quarterly
check-ins. Promotion and compensation decisions are made in the May cycle
and effective July 1.

Performance Improvement Plans are coordinated by HR (E026) and require
30, 60, or 90 day milestones depending on severity. The employee, manager,
and HR are all signatories.

## Reporting concerns

See `code_of_conduct.md` for the escalation paths. The anonymous ethics
hotline is operated by Acme Compliance Services and can be reached at
the URL distributed at hire.

## Acknowledgement

# Regulatory Compliance Memo

Quarterly summary of the regulatory regimes Meridian operates under and
their implications for engineering and security work. Maintained by the
General Counsel's office (E006); operational coordination through the
Trinity program (P014).

## In scope

- **SOC 2 Type II.** Annual audit. Trust Services Criteria for Security,
  Availability, and Confidentiality. Evidence collection led by E033 and
  E042 under Trinity.
- **ISO 27001.** Surveillance audit annually, full re-certification every
  three years. Most recent surveillance audit: 2025-Q4, no major findings.
- **GDPR (EU customers).** Data Protection Officer is currently the GC
  (E006). DPAs required for every EU customer; template owned by E009.
- **CCPA / CPRA (California).** Consumer rights handling routes through
  Legal + People Ops.
- **Breach notification.** State-level matrices vary; Legal maintains a
  jurisdiction map. The IR playbook (`incident_response_playbook.md`)
  defers customer-facing disclosure timing to GC + CEO + CISO together.

## Watchlist

- EU AI Act risk classifications. Meridian's products do not currently
  fall under high-risk categories; we are tracking implementation
  guidance.
- US state-level AI legislation. CA, NY, IL all have proposals in 2026
  sessions.

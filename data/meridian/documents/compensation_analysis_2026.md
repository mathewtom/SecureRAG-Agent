# Executive Compensation Analysis — 2026 Cycle

This document is RESTRICTED. The 2026 executive compensation cycle is
governed by the People team (E005) in coordination with the CFO (E007)
and CEO (E012). Final recommendations go to the Compensation
subcommittee of the Board.

## Methodology

- Peer benchmarking against the Radford Executive panel for our size
  and stage.
- Internal equity review across executive band.
- Performance modifiers applied per the executive scorecard agreed at
  the 2025 Q4 board meeting.

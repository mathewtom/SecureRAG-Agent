1. GDPR Compliance:
- Annual DPA review completed for all 23 EU sub-processors

- One near-miss: Customer data from EU was temporarily replicated to us-west-2 before CloudVault geo-restriction was applied. No breach occurred. Documented in incident log GDPR-2026-003.

2. SOX Compliance:
- Q4 2025 internal controls testing complete. Two deficiencies identified:
  a. Segregation of duties gap in AP approval workflow (Robert Walsh can both create and approve POs under $5,000). Remediated February 15 by reducing single-approval threshold to $2,000.
  b. IT general controls: Shared admin credentials for CloudVault console. Remediated January 30 by migrating to SSO with individual credentials.
